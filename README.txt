# Microwave Battle Arena
**Made during the 2017 Global Game Jam**

_Playing Instructions_
* This is a local multiplayer game. Grab some friends to play!
* Each player is assigned a button from among the following: Spacebar, Q, P, M (and possibly Z)
* Do not panic when you realize you have began the fight while spinning.
* Press your button to change your spin direction.
* Hold your button to move forward (you will not spin while moving forward)
* Double-tap (quickly press twice in succession) your button to open the microwave door (the black line) and shoot a wave.
* Your goal is to reduce the Health bar of all other microwaves to zero (the red bar)
* Have fun, the last microwave standing is the winner!
